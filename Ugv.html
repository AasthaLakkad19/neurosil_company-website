UGV SATHI

UGV SATHI is an intelligent Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) designed to support surveillance, inspection, and rescue operations in hazardous and high-risk environments where direct human involvement is unsafe. The project focuses on improving safety, situational awareness, and operational efficiency during critical missions.

UGV SATHI integrates multiple sensors, real-time video monitoring, and semi-autonomous navigation to detect obstacles and monitor surroundings while operating across rough and uneven terrains. It features live camera streaming and remote/manual control, allowing operators to assess and respond to situations safely from a distance.

Built using embedded controllers and wireless communication modules with a modular hardware architecture, SATHI is scalable, upgrade-friendly, and adaptable for defense, industrial safety, smart infrastructure, and disaster management applications.
