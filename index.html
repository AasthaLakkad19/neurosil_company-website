About

UGV (Unmanned Ground Vehicles) is a robotics initiative focused on building intelligent land-based autonomous systems that enhance safety, efficiency, and innovation across industries.

We specialize in creating robots powered by advanced sensors, AI, precise control systems, and modular designs—from firefighting robots to surveillance and automation.

Our products—UGV-Sathi and YAGYA—represent our commitment to developing robotics solutions that serve society, industry, and emergency environments.
