YAGYA

YAGYA is an intelligent automated Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) designed exclusively for surveillance and monitoring in hazardous and restricted environments where direct human observation is unsafe or impractical. The project emphasizes automated situational awareness, real-time monitoring, and minimal human intervention.

YAGYA employs sensor-driven automation and autonomous navigation to continuously observe its surroundings, detect obstacles, and analyze environmental conditions in real time. Integrated AI-based human detection enables the system to identify human presence, track movement, and distinguish humans from other objects during surveillance operations.

With automated decision-making and live video surveillance, YAGYA provides intelligent scene analysis while navigating complex terrains. Its modular and scalable automated architecture supports deployment in industrial surveillance, smart city monitoring, perimeter security, and defense reconnaissance, ensuring reliable and autonomous surveillance missions.
